About

Trusted Expertise in
Tax & Regulation

Juris Nair is a Mumbai-based legal practice with deep expertise in Indirect Tax and Regulatory Laws.

With a practice rooted in precision and client focus, we provide strategic counsel to businesses of all sizes — from startups navigating GST compliance for the first time to large enterprises managing complex regulatory matters.

The practice covers all aspects of GST, Customs, Foreign Trade Policy, and allied regulatory frameworks — offering end-to-end legal support from advisory and structuring to litigation and representation before authorities.

Practice Areas

⚖️

GST Advisory & Compliance

Comprehensive guidance on Goods and Services Tax — registration, returns, input tax credit optimization, classification, and compliance management for businesses across sectors.

🏛️

GST Litigation

Representation before GST authorities, appellate forums, and tribunals. Drafting show cause notice replies, appeals, and managing departmental audits and assessments.

🚢

Customs & Foreign Trade

Expert counsel on Customs Act, Import-Export Policy, Foreign Trade Policy (FTP), advance authorizations, EPCG, and customs valuation disputes.

📋

Regulatory Consulting

Strategic advisory on allied regulatory frameworks — FEMA, SEZ, anti-dumping, and sector-specific compliance — helping businesses stay ahead of regulatory change.

🔍

Tax Due Diligence

Indirect tax due diligence for mergers, acquisitions, and business restructuring — identifying risks, liabilities, and opportunities before transactions close.

🎓

Training & Workshops

Corporate training sessions and workshops on GST, Customs, and regulatory matters — empowering your finance and compliance teams with practical knowledge.
