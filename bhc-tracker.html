How this works: A scheduled job runs every evening at 7:30 PM IST, fetches the latest judgments from all five BHC benches, classifies each one, and publishes the results to this page. No clicks needed from you -- just visit and view.

Confidence levels: HIGH two or more strong signals   MEDIUM one strong signal   LOW generic tax keywords -- manual review recommended.

Export: The "Export to CSV" button downloads the filtered view in a format ready to paste into your existing register.

Manual refresh: You can trigger an update anytime by going to the Actions tab of your GitHub repository and clicking "Run workflow" on "BHC Daily Orders Scraper".
